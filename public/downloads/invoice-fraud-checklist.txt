25 red flags to check before approving any invoice
================================================

BEFORE YOU APPROVE ANY INVOICE, CHECK:

VENDOR IDENTITY VERIFICATION
[ ] 1.  The vendor email domain matches the company website exactly
        RED FLAG: billing@g00gle-services.com when the company is googleservices.com
        RED FLAG: typosquatted domains (acme-invoices.com instead of acme.com)
        RED FLAG: free email address used for billing (Gmail, Yahoo, Hotmail)

[ ] 2.  The vendor exists in your approved vendor list
        RED FLAG: First invoice from a vendor you have not onboarded
        ACTION: Call the vendor on a known phone number to verify before paying

[ ] 3.  The vendor phone number is consistent with prior invoices
        RED FLAG: New phone number added to invoice with no prior notice

[ ] 4.  The physical address matches what is on file
        RED FLAG: PO Box only (no street address) for a company claiming to provide physical services
        RED FLAG: Residential address for a supposedly large vendor

AMOUNT VERIFICATION
[ ] 5.  The amount is consistent with the contract or purchase order
        RED FLAG: Invoice exceeds approved PO amount by any amount without a change order

[ ] 6.  Check for round-number manipulation
        RED FLAG: Amount is exactly $1,000 / $5,000 / $10,000 / $25,000 with no line-item breakdown
        NOTE: Real invoices for services have specific amounts ($4,837.50 not $5,000.00)

[ ] 7.  Line items add up to the total correctly
        RED FLAG: Math errors in line items (common in fraudulent invoices)

[ ] 8.  Tax amounts are realistic for the jurisdiction
        RED FLAG: Tax rate does not match the vendor's state/country

URGENCY & PRESSURE TACTICS
[ ] 9.  Invoice does not demand immediate or same-day payment
        RED FLAG: "Pay immediately or service will be suspended"
        RED FLAG: "This offer expires in 24 hours"
        RED FLAG: "Failure to pay will result in legal action"
        NOTE: Legitimate vendors give Net 30 or agreed payment terms — not ultimatums

[ ] 10. The invoice arrived through normal channels, not only by email
        RED FLAG: Invoice arrived with a follow-up call pressuring fast payment

[ ] 11. The person requesting urgent payment is someone you can verify independently
        RED FLAG: "Our CEO has approved this — pay today" from someone you have never spoken to

BANK ACCOUNT CHANGES
[ ] 12. No bank account change notice appears on this invoice
        RED FLAG: "Please note our bank details have changed" in any invoice or email
        RED FLAG: New bank routing or account number you have not seen before
        ACTION: Call the vendor's main phone number (from your records, not from the email)
                to confirm any bank account change before processing

[ ] 13. Bank routing number is valid for the claimed bank
        Check: U.S. routing numbers are 9 digits; verify at ABA routing number lookup

[ ] 14. International SWIFT/IBAN matches the vendor's known country
        RED FLAG: A U.S. vendor suddenly providing a foreign bank account

INVOICE DOCUMENT QUALITY
[ ] 15. Invoice has a unique invoice number in the vendor's sequence
        RED FLAG: INV-0001 or INV-001 (starts at 1) from a vendor you have worked with for years

[ ] 16. Invoice number is not duplicated (check your payment records)
        RED FLAG: Same invoice number you have already paid

[ ] 17. Invoice date is not in the future or more than 90 days in the past
        RED FLAG: Invoice dated 6 months ago appearing for the first time

[ ] 18. Logos and formatting match previous invoices from this vendor
        RED FLAG: Different font, logo quality, or layout than prior invoices

APPROVAL WORKFLOW
[ ] 19. Invoice has been approved by the appropriate budget authority
        Standard: Under $1,000 = manager; $1,000-$10,000 = director; over $10,000 = VP/CFO

[ ] 20. Invoice matches a purchase order in your system
        RED FLAG: No PO number on invoices over your PO threshold

[ ] 21. Work or delivery has been confirmed by the receiving department
        RED FLAG: Paying for services before they are confirmed received

[ ] 22. This vendor has not had a bank account change in the past 90 days
        Check your vendor management records before processing any payment

DIGITAL INVOICE SAFETY
[ ] 23. PDF invoice was opened in a sandboxed viewer — not in a browser plugin
        RED FLAG: PDF asks to enable macros or run a script

[ ] 24. Links in the email point to the vendor's actual domain
        RED FLAG: "View invoice" link goes to a different domain than the vendor

[ ] 25. Email headers confirm the email actually originated from the vendor's domain
        Check: Email client's "View original" or "Show headers" to see SPF/DKIM pass status
        RED FLAG: "From" shows vendor name but "Reply-To" is a different address

================================================
If you find 3+ flags, DO NOT PAY. Contact the vendor via a verified phone number.
If you suspect BEC fraud, contact your bank immediately — wire transfers cannot be recalled after 24 hours.
